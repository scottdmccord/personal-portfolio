About Me

Scott is currently enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. After seven exhilarating years with the social enterprise, Building Markets, Scott is pursuing his passion for technology and plans to work as a web developer beginning in early 2017.

In the summer of 2011, he was transferred to Port-au-Prince, Haiti, where he continued his market research duties and assumed responsibility of project operations and information systems.
